# 🌾 Aruvadai Aran – Smart Agri Storage & Governance Platform

## 🚨 Problem

During the last harvest season, due to limited warehouse availability, harvested crops had to be stored temporarily in open areas using tarpaulin sheets. Unexpected rainfall led to significant crop damage before they could be transported to government procurement centers.

This highlights a critical gap in real-time storage visibility, coordination, and infrastructure planning within the agricultural supply chain. Farmers often lack timely access to information about available storage facilities, leading to avoidable post-harvest losses.

---

## 💡 Solution

**Aruvadai Aran** is a smart web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and storage infrastructure by providing:

* 📍 Real-time warehouse availability tracking
* 🏢 Visibility of government storage facilities
* 🔔 Alerts for storage availability and updates
* 🤝 Farmer–Government coordination interface
* 🌐 Bilingual support (Tamil + English)

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Reduce post-harvest crop losses
* Improve farmer income stability
* Enable efficient public storage utilization
* Enhance transparency in government procurement systems
